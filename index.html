TweetRide

TweetRide fetches tweets about highways and transit systems in Toronto (@680newstraffic, @TTCnotices ). Then, it organizes each tweet by the highways it is referring to as well as the content it has. The tweets are then displayed in a table with a indication beside the tweet indicating delays, construction,collision etc.

fitPlanner

fitPlanner is an exercise progress tracker. This app is directed for beginners at the gym. This app allows you to view and analyze your performance for a single workout and/or a workout schedule. It is an alternative to using pen and paper to keep track of your exercise.


fitPlanner saves all your workout data so no need to worry about gym track sheet getting destroyed or lost. This app has a performance history page where you can see all the previous workout data including sets, reps and weights.
